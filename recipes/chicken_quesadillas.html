Chicken quesadillas

Home

Description

This chicken quesadilla recipe is great to make for parties. Zesty chicken, cooked peppers, and melted cheese are a delightful combination. Cut into wedges and serve with sour cream and salsa.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb diced, boneless, skinless chicken breast
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 1 green bell pepper
  • 3 cups shredded chedder cheese
  • 10 (10 inch) flour tortillas

Steps

  1. Step 1

    Season the chicken, then broil it until itâ€™s cooked through.

  2. Step 2

    Cook the vegetables with the broiled chicken.

  3. Step 3

    Layer the mixture onto half of the tortilla, then top with cheese.

  4. Step 4

    Fold the tortillas and bake until the cheese is melted.

  5. Step 5

    Cut the quesadillas into wedges.
